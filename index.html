My story

The part I loved was never the machines.

I studied industrial engineering, but what pulled me in was never the factory floor. It was the people, and the moment a messy process turns into something that just works.

At RewU I co-founded a rewards platform and ran with it: the strategy, the go-to-market, the partnerships that brought on 50 companies, and the product itself, hands-on. I can happily spend a whole day on how one screen should feel for the person on the other side, where my "how will they feel" instinct and a builder's "what is realistic" meet, and become better than either of us pictured.

At Upgraide I sit with clients, map how their business actually runs, find where the time leaks out, and design the solution that gives it back. I own the relationship and the discovery, and the work gets built from there.

I am 25 and still finding the exact thing I will obsess over. But I know its shape: close to customers, close to a product, with people I like. When something genuinely moves me, I do not really stop thinking about it.
